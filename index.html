What is NIP-05 for Nostr?

Your Identity

The NIP-05 Nostr protocol employs a human-readable identifier to easily find and follow others.

Easily Reconizable

The identifier serves as a substitute for a public key and identifies users in a recognizable way.

Personalized Address

This identifier is commonly compared to an email address you can personalize: username@verified-nostr.com
